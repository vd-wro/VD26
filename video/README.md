# Robot Driving Demonstration Videos

Here are videos demonstrating our robot's autonomous driving capabilities for the WRO competition.

---

## Open Challenge Round Video

This video illustrates our robot's **MPU-6050 with PID orientation control**, coupled with **ultrasonic trajectory correction**.

For the Open Round, we adjusted our strategy because touching the outermost wall is prohibited. The robot's behavior in this video differs slightly from the complete code's. This adaptation is specifically for the Panamanian regional rounds, where the **center circuit configuration remains consistent**. In the full code, the robot prioritizes staying closer to the outermost wall to adapt to various environments.

[![Open Challenge Video](https://img.youtube.com/vi/nUoTaL3pmQw/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nUoTaL3pmQw)

Previous versions of the robot share the same Open Round code and behavior; however, ViZio V3 now is able to navigate trough tighter spaces with the variable center.

[![Obstacle Challenge Video1](https://img.youtube.com/vi/Y4y8UubQgvo/0.jpg)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y4y8UubQgvo)

## Obstacle Challenge Round Video

This video primarily showcases our robot's **artificial vision capabilities** using the **PixyCam 2.1**. It also features trajectory correction based on the MPU and PID control, supplemented by intentionally colliding with the outermost wall for a hard correction. If you observe closely, you'll also notice the subtle ultrasonic correction, which ensures the PixyCam consistently detects obstacles and reacts appropriately.
